Game Description:

In this game, your mission will be to explore the Wumpus World in search for gold.

You will start in the bottom left square (0,0) equipped with a bow and one arrow.

In a single room of the cave, sits a immobile Wumpus, ready for dinner

Some rooms of the cave my have a deep slimy pit that will kill players who slip into its depth

How to play Hunt the Wumpus

Points

  • -1 point for each action taken
  • -10 points for shooting the arrow
  • -1000 points for being eaten by the wumpus or falling into a pit
  • -2000 points for starving (after 160 actions without death)
  • +1000 points for escaping the cave with the gold

Player Actions

  • Move Left: (A Key)
  • Move Right: (D Key)
  • Move Up: (W Key)
  • Move Down: (S Key)
  • Shoot Arrow: (Arrow keys decide direction)
  • Pick up Gold: (Enter Key)
  • Climb Out: (C Key)

Game Parameters

  • One Wumpus in a random room other than (0,0)
  • Pits randomly placed, with 20% probability in each room other than (0,0)
  • Gold in a random room
